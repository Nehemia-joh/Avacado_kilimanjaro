A Sanctuary at the Foot of Africa's Icon

Nestled within a vibrant avocado plantation, our lodge provides breathtaking panoramic views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Every detail, from our architecture to our operations, embodies a deep commitment to sustainability and harmony with the pristine natural environment.

Our dedicated team is passionate about crafting unforgettable journeys. Whether it's a guided walk through indigenous forests, an exhilarating cultural exchange with local communities, or simply unwinding by the pool with unparalleled views, we prioritize responsible tourism that enriches both our guests and the ecosystem.

Culinary Journeys that Delight the Senses

Our passionate chefs artfully blend Tanzania's vibrant culinary traditions with international techniques, creating innovative dishes that tell a story of the land. We take pride in our direct-from-farm sourcing, ensuring every bite is a celebration of purity and flavor.

Whether you prefer a romantic candlelit dinner under the vast African sky with Kilimanjaro looming majestically, or a spirited bush breakfast amidst the sounds of nature, our diverse dining experiences are designed to enchant. Complement your meal with a selection from our exquisite wine cellar, featuring fine vintages from South Africa and beyond.
