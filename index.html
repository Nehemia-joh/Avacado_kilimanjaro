A Sanctuary at the Foot of Kilimanjaro

Nestled in a lush avocado orchard with panoramic views of Mount Kilimanjaro, our lodge offers a unique blend of luxury and wilderness. Each element of our design reflects our commitment to sustainability and harmony with the natural environment.

Our passionate team is dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences, from guided nature walks to cultural immersions with local communities. We believe in responsible tourism that benefits both our guests and the surrounding ecosystem.

A Taste of Tanzania

Our talented chefs create inspired dishes that celebrate Tanzania's rich culinary heritage while incorporating international techniques. We source ingredients from our own organic gardens and local farmers, ensuring peak freshness and flavor.

Dine under the stars on our terrace with Kilimanjaro as your backdrop, or enjoy a private bush dinner in a secluded location on the lodge grounds. Our extensive wine list features the best of South African vineyards alongside international selections.
